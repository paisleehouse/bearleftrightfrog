GODZILLA

the atom bomb and me April 12, 2024

Years ago, I watched many more movies than I do today. One of the ways that I watched movies was picking a director or series and consuming everything I could. As 2024 has unfolded, I have wnated to focus my attendion in a considered way. Less mindless consumption and scrolling, more concentrated efforts at enjoyment. 2023 was a year of destruction and disccovery. I had been in therapy for a year. Learning and unlearning things about myself and the world around me. Discovering that I love hiking, music, yoga, books, learning, and so many other things. Destroying a persistent need to avoid and run away from people and difficult things. Ending a ten year relationshop and marriage. In the midst of all of this change, I found myself missing the way I used to seek out movies. I resolved to retry old ways. What better place to start in a celebration discovery, destruction, and new life than the Godzilla movies.
